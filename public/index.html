Do you love your golf?

So do we!

Do you love prizes?

With competitions on both Saturday and Sunday mornings and with regular multiple week major competitions, there are heaps of opportunities to win!
Prizes range from golf balls to Pro shop vouchers to trophies

Looking for a hobby both you and your partner can enjoy?

Wattle Park Golf Club offers both mens and ladies competitions so come along and if you have small children, tell the grand parents we are sure that they wouldn't mind taking care of them for the morning.

Always looking for somone to play golf with?

We understand that not everyone loves the game of golf (even if we dont understand why). If you find you are lacking appropriately (golf) minded friends then just come down to the club and we will introduce you to lots.

Want to gain an official handicap?

A handicap system levels the field so that all players, regardless of skill, can play competitively against one another. That's great! we all have a chance of winning i hear you say, but it gets better. Not only do you have a chance of winning at Wattle Park but also any other event you wish to enter outside of the club!
